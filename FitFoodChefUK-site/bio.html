Joe Gould

Award-winning British chef • Michelin background • Fitness-driven flavour specialist

Joe Gould is an award-winning British chef with over 17 years of experience leading some of the UK’s most respected luxury hotel kitchens. Currently Head Chef at Fawsley Hall Hotel & Spa, Joe oversees a 65-bedroom historic property with multiple dining outlets, a brigade of 16 chefs, and a modern seasonal menu shaped around strong commercial performance and uncompromising culinary standards.

Before joining Fawsley Hall, Joe served as Executive Chef at the Relais & Châteaux Glenapp Castle, where he led the culinary programme across fine dining, private penthouse service, outdoor sea-safari cooking, large-scale corporate events, and high-profile international occasions including COP26. His food and leadership featured in multiple national television programmes throughout 2021–2023.

Earlier in his career, Joe earned three consecutive AA Rosettes as Head Chef at Fishmore Hall Hotel & Spa and refined his modern British style at Lainston House. He was also part of the acclaimed kitchen team at Coworth Park (Restaurant John Campbell) that was awarded a Michelin star, contributing to one of the most technically respected restaurants in the country at the time.

Alongside his hotel and restaurant work, Joe has also cooked for McLaren’s Formula One team as Jenson Button’s private chef, served as an exhibition chef for the North American Olive Oil Conventions, and worked with Rhubarb Food Design on prestigious events including Ascot’s private boxes and major corporate openings.

Joe is a former Chaine des Rôtisseurs UK Young Chef of the Year and has completed stages across Michelin-starred kitchens throughout Europe. Today, he brings this wealth of classical and modern technique to Fit Food UK — helping people elevate their nutrition, master restaurant-level flavour, and build a lifestyle that supports strength, performance, and real-world sustainability.
