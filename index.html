Bio

I’m a professional chef with 20+ years in high-end hospitality, operating at 3 AA Rosette level. Fit Food means fit-for-purpose: designing food operations around the reality of your space, staff, service and budget.

What you’re really paying for

Judgement, delivery, and an operation that works beyond opening week — not “ideas” or generic PDFs.

  • On-site observation and workflow stress testing
  • Practical constraints applied before you spend money
  • Decisions that survive real staffing and service conditions

Where I help that AI can’t

AI is useful for options. It can’t see your reality.

  • I physically assess your space and movement
  • I spot friction points you didn’t think to ask about
  • I design for the people you can actually hire

Food Business Startups

You’ve got the idea. Now it needs to be runnable — with real staff, real service pressure, real suppliers and real constraints.

Typical work

  • Feasibility + reality check before you commit
  • Menu scope and complexity control
  • Workflow design and service sequencing
  • Staffing skill ceiling and training plan

Outcome

  • A food operation that fits your model and budget
  • Fewer expensive mistakes baked in
  • Clarity on what to do — and what to avoid

Adding a Food Offering

Food should support the business — not dominate it. The goal is a controlled offer that fits your space, staffing and flow.

Typical work

  • Define the offer (and its limits)
  • Service model and operational flow
  • Equipment specification that earns its footprint
  • Supplier + procurement guidance

Outcome

  • A lean, stable food system
  • Reduced compliance and staffing risk
  • Food that fits your brand without breaking operations

New Management

Taking over an existing food operation? The fastest wins come from stabilising standards and workflow — then tightening margins and training.

Typical work

  • Rapid operational assessment
  • Workflow fixes and standards reset
  • Menu scope correction (remove what breaks service)
  • Training and checks that hold without you

Outcome

  • Stability fast — less chaos, more control
  • Clear priorities and an achievable plan
  • Practical systems staff can actually follow
