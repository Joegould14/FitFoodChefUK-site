Fit Food Consultancy

With two decades of professional experience across luxury hotels, fine-dining restaurants, large-scale banqueting, live demonstrations, and private work for elite clients—including F1 drivers—I offer hospitality businesses a practical, senior-level perspective on how to elevate standards while protecting margins.

My consultancy focuses on the areas that actually move the needle:

  • Food Offering & Menu Development
    Refining dishes, pricing, plating, workflow, and consistency to improve both guest experience and GP.
  • Operational Efficiency & Payroll Control
    Building sustainable rotas, reducing labour waste, and restructuring kitchen/FOH processes so the operation runs lean without losing quality.
  • Training: Kitchen & Front of House
    Clear, actionable training that raises skill levels, service standards, communication, and accountability across the whole team.
  • Health, Safety, Dietary & Allergen Compliance
    Robust systems that protect your guests and your business, avoiding the gaps that most venues don’t notice until it’s too late.
  • Future-Proofing Through AI Integration
    Practical, hospitality-specific AI tools and workflows to streamline admin, improve menu costing, tighten compliance, reduce food waste, and support management decision-making.

If you’re a hospitality owner, GM, or head chef looking for someone who understands the realities of a commercial kitchen, the expectations of luxury clientele, and the future direction of the industry, I can help you sharpen your operation—without disrupting what already works.
